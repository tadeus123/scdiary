A Skillcashe is a seperate section in the humanoid robots brain that is reserved for learning skills instantly and storing them for fast use. The skillcashe does not change the foundation model of the humanoid robot, but stores specific and 100% individual skills that are acquired by observing, listening and sensing with all possible sense of the humanoid. The skillcashe can be activated on commmand or in later humanoid version will be activated by the main foundational model when it recognizes oppertunities to learn. The skillcashe is a seperate section that interacts with the main model just as beeing there as knowledge. The main foundational model can acess the skillcashe to performed individual learned tasks. In our skill cashe this skills get acquired based on sensing and observating and then can be used from the main foundational model like cursor agent can use the skill of taking a screenshot. The skillcashe is more a databank for a certain action that is stored in a seperate set of weights and don't interfear with the main foundational model. The skill cashe has a limit of how many skills are able to get stored. The skill cashe does store actions in a very highly and data rich format so that a action get be imitaded very accurately. The skillcashe does not actively communicate with the main foundational model, moreover the foundational model cann use the skillcashe if needed and acces it and give it instructions to perform the skill "cook me my favourite breakfest eggs" or "cut my hair as usial". Other ways to make MOF-303:

The grinding way (what we talked about before): You mix and grind the stuff by hand or with a machine. It's easy for small amounts and uses mostly water.

The heating way (called reflux): You mix everything in water, heat it up to boiling in a pot with a lid that catches the steam, and stir for 4–8 hours. This makes bigger amounts (like 100 g or more) and is good for growing nice crystals. You need a hot plate and a simple setup to catch the steam.

The sealed heating way (like in a pressure cooker): You put the mix in a strong sealed bottle or metal can, heat it to 100–150°C for a few hours or days. This is for even bigger batches but needs safe tools to handle the pressure.

Microwave way — You mix the stuff in water and put it in a microwave oven for just a few minutes (like 5–10 min). It heats very fast and makes the powder quickly. Needs a special microwave-safe container, but it's one of the fastest methods.

Thought influence -> stored in the subconscious -> triggers a thought.
